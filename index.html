PT. Agrakom Para Relatika (AgrakomPR) is an Indonesia independent local agency that was established in 1997. We have been trusted to handle blue chip clients in retainer and ad-hoc basis with 10 – 15 clients annually. Our team comes from various educational backgrounds including Communications, Law, Economics, Journalism, Engineering and Agriculture that makes it easier to handle clients with diverse business and interests. We believe in our work values: Commitment, Creativity, Flexibility, Result and Honesty, everytime we do our job, together with Zest and Enthusiasm for the account as our work spirit. We have a good understanding on local market, network in Java, Sumatera, Kalimantan, Bali, as well as a strong relationship with media across all industries.
